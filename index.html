About Charlady

Charlady is a specialized online platform connecting Kenyan housekeepers, nannies, and domestic workers with employers seeking reliable household assistance. Designed with a user-friendly approach, Charlady ensures a seamless experience for both job seekers and employers while maintaining high standards of professionalism and trust.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between households and skilled domestic workers by offering a secure and efficient job-matching platform that enhances employment opportunities and household management.

Our Vision

To become Kenya's most trusted and efficient platform for hiring domestic workers, ensuring professionalism, safety, and convenience for all users.

Key Features

  • ✔ Worker Profiles & Job Applications – Nannies and housekeepers create detailed profiles and apply for jobs.
  • ✔ Employer Job Postings – Households post job openings with salary details, location, and requirements.
  • ✔ Manual Verification – Admin manually verifies workers and job postings before they go live.
  • ✔ Dashboard for Users – Employers and workers track applications, job postings, and payments.
  • ✔ Secure Payment & Contracts – Workers pay a membership fee for verification and receive access to contract terms.
  • ✔ Ratings & Reviews – Employers can rate workers based on performance, creating a transparent system.
  • ✔ Mobile-Friendly & Elegant UI – Designed with a feminine touch using high-quality visuals and smooth UX/UI.

Why Choose Charlady?

  • ✅ Trusted & Verified Workers – Each worker goes through a verification process before being listed.
  • ✅ Seamless Hiring Process – Employers find housekeepers easily without the hassle of agencies.
  • ✅ Transparent Ratings & Reviews – No moderation, ensuring honest employer and worker feedback.
  • ✅ Secure & Professional Platform – No direct communication outside the platform, ensuring a controlled hiring process.
